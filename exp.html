My Experiences Upto 12th

Namaskar,
My name is Vishal Jaiswal. I was born on 20 August 2002, at a very small village Khurhuriya at barhni block of district Siddharth Nagar in U.P.,India We are three brothers and two sisters.My father does agriculture. I feel very very proud that i was born on the lap of BHARAT MATA.


I want to share my educational Experiences upto class 12th: I completed my early education from my parents and a private school named ,Shreemati Shyama Devi Shishu Mandir and junior school "Vijay Surya Bal Vidya Mandir" located at 1 km away from my hoiuse at village Dudhawaniya Khurd. There had very beautiful memories and I wish that days come again.There were 60 students,I had good friendship with me and everyone. Everyone had alot of fun,eating together and live happily with the support of teachers.


The most important thing about this school was that along with education,emphasis was given on the all around development of children. I still remember that I did many different roles in various cultural events like Annual Function,National festivals etc. I had played the role of LAKSHMANA in Ramayan Episodes,comic roles,in singing group dancing and acting also. I learned a lot from this school and the art of living life.


After the completion of junior education,i got my admission in the most famous and no.1 U.P. board school in my distict named "Raghuwar Prasad Jaiswal Saraswati Shishu Mandir tetari bazar,Siddharth Nagar". I got my Higher Secondary and Intermediate education from here by staying at aunt's house. In our school,there was alot of emphasis on descipline as well as told about our culture, morality and inspiration to follow it in life.

In High school Board examination 2018, I topped in entire district Siddharth Nagar due to whichi got the opportunity to be rewarded by Chief minister of my state "Mr. Yogi Aadityanath" in Lucknow. This the very beautiful experience for me in whole school life.I passed the intermediate exam with 81.6 % from PCM Now a days I am doing my graduation course B.Tech (Computer Science and Engineering) in Dr. Ram Manohar Lohiya AWadh Universitya Ayodhya U.P.
